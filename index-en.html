Corporate Philosophy

"Creating New Values, Crafting a Refined Future" At Uten Co., Ltd., we harmonize with modern diverse lifestyles, presenting exceptional values to society through uncompromising quality and our unique aesthetic perspective.

While maintaining a global vision, we deeply cherish the meticulous sense of Japanese beauty. Committed to continuous innovation and constant challenges, we aim to deliver subtle abundance and sustainable brilliance to everyday life through our cross-border operations.

Core Businesses

■ Global Investment Consulting Business

We provide comprehensive consulting services for high-net-worth and affluent international investors (primarily based in China) regarding real estate acquisition (land and residential properties) within Japan.

Our firm maintains strategic partnerships with prominent enterprises in China, including "Shanghai Zhouxing Corporate Management Consulting Co., Ltd." and "Shanghai Yulu Yun Information Technology Co., Ltd." Upon the successful launch of domestic investment projects, we offer seamless local asset management and property operation services in Japan.

Furthermore, moving beyond simple transactions, we support the maximization of investment values through expert, full-cycle execution—spanning from land acquisition planning to the development and ultimate operation of boutique hotels and premium apartments.

■ Jewelry & E-Commerce Business

Launched in January 2026. Rooted in rigorous pearl selection standards and our sophisticated "Mode Style" design philosophy, we manage the complete creation, manufacturing, cross-border e-commerce, and import/export trade of our house pearl jewelry brand. We deliver a modern pearl aesthetic that embodies quiet elegance and understated maturity on a global scale.
